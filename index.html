## About

I'm a systems administrator turned DevOps engineer with 7+ years of hands-on experience running secure Linux and Windows environments. At Slice Labs I've worn both hats — three years owning IT and security operations (SOC 2 readiness, SIEM monitoring, incident response), then moving to DevOps to manage AWS infrastructure, EKS access control, and CI/CD automation.

I like infrastructure that is boring in the best way: patched, monitored, documented, and recoverable.
